Wyman's Baby Berry

Based in DownEast Maine, Wyman's has a simple mission of helping the world eat more fruit. They have impeccably maintain their barrens of antioxidant-rich Wild Blueberries and partnered with like-minded farmers from all over. In fall 2022, for my Design Thinking class, we consulted for Wyman's and came up with a new product that they could come up for their brand.
